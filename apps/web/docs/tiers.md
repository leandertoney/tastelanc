## What Is TasteLanc?

TasteLanc is Lancaster's hyper local dining discovery app that brings customers directly to your restaurant.

**Tagline:** Eat. Drink. Experience!

---

## Why Restaurants Love TasteLanc + Rosie

1. **Hyper-Local = Higher Intent**
2. **Verified + Real-Time Information**
3. **Rosie Drives Discovery**
   - New Customers
   - Frequent Visits
   - More Revenue Opportunities
   - More Affordable than Ads

## Rosie

Rosie helps users find:
- Your menu
- Your weekly specials
- Your happy hours
- Your events & entertainment
- Your best dishes
— all within seconds.

**She is your friendly digital host, walking customers right to your door.**

## Dashboard Access by Tier

### Basic Tier (Free)
- `/dashboard` - Main overview
- `/dashboard/profile` - Basic info only (name, address, phone, website, description)
- `/dashboard/hours` - Business hours
- `/dashboard/photos` - Cover photo only
- `/dashboard/subscription` - Upgrade page

### Premium Tier ($200-650)
Everything in Basic, plus:
- `/dashboard/menu` - Full menu management
- `/dashboard/analytics` - Consumer analytics
- `/dashboard/specials` - Weekly specials
- `/dashboard/happy-hours` - Happy hour management
- `/dashboard/events` - Entertainment/events
- Logo upload in profile

### Elite Tier ($300-1100)
Everything in Premium, plus:
- Logo displayed on map
- Advanced analytics dashboard
- Daily special list prominence
- Social media content creation
- Event spotlights
- Live entertainment spotlights
- Weekly update service
